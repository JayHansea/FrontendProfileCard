About Me

My Bio

I am a FullStack Software Engineer with a journey rooted in a deep fascination for technology's power to solve real-world problems. With extensive experience from creating responsive UIs with React and Next.js to building robust backends with Node.js and MongoDB. I thrive on transforming complex ideas into performant and scalable applications.
